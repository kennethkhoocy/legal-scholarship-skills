\section{Design}
\label{sec:design}

The design compares records before and after a reporting template is introduced.
The estimand is written as
\begin{equation}
\Delta = E[Y_i \mid T_i=1] - E[Y_i \mid T_i=0],
\label{eq:estimand}
\end{equation}
where $T_i$ denotes exposure to the template. Equation~\ref{eq:estimand} provides
a compact reference target for the appendix.
